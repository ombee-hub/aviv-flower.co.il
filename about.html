About Aviv

Growers first.
Everything else follows.

Aviv began in the fields — and it still starts there every morning. What grew from a single Israeli farm is today an international supply operation, but the measure of quality never changed: the stem in the buyer's hand.

Our story

Four decades on the clock

  • 1988

    Founded in Israel

    Aviv is established as a grower-exporter of cut flowers, selling through the Dutch auction system from day one.

  • 1990s

    The HARMONIE brand

    Specialty crops and hard-to-find varieties are gathered under one quality mark — HARMONIE — recognized on the auction clock.

  • 2000s

    Europe, in person

    Subsidiaries open in the Netherlands and Germany (Aviv GmbH), putting our own people next to the customers they serve.

  • 2010s

    Fresh produce joins

    The cold chain built for flowers starts carrying culinary herbs, edible flowers and mini-citrus to European food service.

  • Today

    Direct to the buyer

    The Aviv WebShop connects registered wholesale buyers straight to the grower — live availability, Buy-Now pricing, same-morning photos.

Our growers

Aviv works with a network of partner growers across Israel's growing regions — from the Sharon plain to the Arava desert. Each farm is contracted to HARMONIE grading specs, and each carton is traceable back to its grower and plot.

Vision & goals

Where we're heading

Our vision is simple to state and hard to do: be the supplier that wholesale buyers trust for the varieties nobody else can deliver reliably — and make buying them as direct and transparent as walking the field yourself.

That means investing in three things: specialty breeding partnerships that keep the HARMONIE catalogue ahead of the market; cold-chain and logistics technology that shortens the hours between field and vase; and digital trade — the WebShop and grower portal — that removes middlemen without removing accountability.

Aviv Germany

Aviv GmbH serves wholesalers, garden centers and retail chains across Germany and Central Europe with daily deliveries from the Aalsmeer hub — a local team, local invoicing and the full HARMONIE range.
