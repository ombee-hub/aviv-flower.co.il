About · Our operations

From field to flight, in one day.

Everything between the grower's field and the buyer's cooler runs through our packing house: receiving, grading, hydration, quality control, packing and air-freight consolidation — all under one roof, all inside one working day.

The day, hour by hour

One working day, one cold chain

  • 06:00 · FIELD

    Harvest in the cool hours

    Growers cut at first light and move stems into hydration within minutes. Produce lines are picked to the same clock.

  • 08:00 · RECEIVING

    Arrival at the packing house

    Every delivery is logged against its grower and plot, checked for temperature and moved straight into pre-cooling.

  • 10:00 · GRADING

    HARMONIE grading & QC

    Stems are graded to the HARMONIE spec sheet — length, maturity, uniformity — and quality control signs off every batch.

  • 14:00 · PACKING

    Packed to order

    Auction lots, WebShop orders and program cartons are packed and labelled, including retail-ready sleeves on request.

  • 22:00 · TLV

    Night flight north

    Consolidated air freight leaves in refrigerated ULDs and lands in Europe before the morning auction.

Quality
