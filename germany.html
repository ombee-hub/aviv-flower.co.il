About · Aviv Germany

Israeli fields,
German mornings.

Aviv Agrarprodukte GmbH, founded in 2013 as the German subsidiary of the Aviv packing house, is based at the VRM flower auction in Herongen (halls M950/951). Cut flowers and greens land fresh from Israel every night and are prepared for sale the same morning.

What we offer

Range & service

Contact the German team

The range covers cut flowers, cut greens, potted plants, fresh herbs and fruit from Israeli production, complemented by locally grown cut flowers. Incoming goods are checked for quality and quantity by our own QC team before they are offered — by phone, WhatsApp, WebShop, Facebook and mail — to wholesale customers across Germany, the Netherlands and Belgium.

SERVICE 01

Clock buying

Purchasing at the auction clock on your behalf, including third-party goods.

SERVICE 02

Consolidation

Commissioning, repacking and load consolidation with piece and quality control.

SERVICE 03

Logistics care

Empties management and carrier notification — the details handled for you.
